User Experience is key
How customers interact with you matters. It's the Alpha and Omega of your business, if you want to retain any. Your current organization and technical setup might not serve to that end, though.
Speed is critical
Bottlenecks and technical debt are holding you back. Dealing with these constraints is still just as important as being innovative and productive. In fact, not taking care will for sure add inertia. Block you entirely, eventually.
Get over projects, build and take care of products
Projects have a definite start and end. Great. Software is living, breathing and always in change though and coming back to user experience never predictable in the way it will play out. So don't stick to rigid, play-safe methods just because they "work". They don't. Play it agile.
